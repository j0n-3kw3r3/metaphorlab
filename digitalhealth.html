Digital Health Kiosk 

Revolutionizing Health Monitoring 

The Metaphor Health Kiosk is a state-of-the-art digital health solution that brings comprehensive health monitoring to the forefront of patient care. Designed with ease of use and accuracy in mind, the kiosk provides monitoring of physiological health indicators, offering insights into a patient’s health status in real-time.

Vital Signs Monitoring

The kiosk is equipped to monitor key health parameters, including temperature, blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen levels, and BMI. It gives healthcare providers a holistic view of a patient’s health.

User-Friendly Interface

An intuitive touch screen makes the kiosk easy to use for patients of all ages and technological backgrounds. 

Immediate Feedback

Patients receive instantaneous results with clear explanations and recommendations that help them understand and stay engaged with their health

Integration with Mobile App

The kiosk is seamlessly integrated with our mobile app for users to access their health data anytime, anywhere. This integration makes health monitoring convenient and accessible. 

Data Management and Privacy 

Client-Controlled Data Management

Data collected by the kiosk is managed by the healthcare institution or hospital to retain control over their patient’s information. 

Privacy Compliance

Patient data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and security. 

Secure and Efficient Handling of Health Data

We understand the critical importance of data privacy and security in healthcare. Our digital health solutions are designed to ensure that all health data collected through our kiosks adheres to stringent data protection regulations.
