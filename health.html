Our Kind of Data

Comprehensive RWE

We gather evidence by tracking patient prognosis, medication usage, clinical events, quality of life metrics, and survival rates (progression free, overall and RECIST for solid tumors).

Diverse Therapeutic Coverage

Our services provide crucial insights into a range of therapeutic areas with high unmet needs in Africa, such as oncology, infectious diseases, and women’s health.

Pricing and Value Assessment

In today’s value-driven healthcare environment, demonstrating the economic benefits of your products is crucial. Our services provide the financial insights needed to support market access and pricing strategies for sustainable revenue.

Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

Willingness-to-Pay Studies 

Understanding the maximum amount that patients or sponsors are willing to pay for your product is essential for pricing strategies. Our studies offer real-world evidence into the perceived value of your product.
